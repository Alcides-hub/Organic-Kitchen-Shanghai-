Welcome to the neighborhood

What fueled us is the belief that food should taste good and do you good. We purchase our products from passionate local organic growers carefully sourced while keeping our environmental impact and food miles super-low. we are not just about organic produce we are passionate for foods of tastes from culinary delights of countries we have traveled. Our dishes and ingredients are carefully selected and tested to make sure that we are delivering authentic tastes from the great dishes we love. What really made us believe we were on the right track was the passion and support of people who eat with us, but the foremost message was that you are the source of our inspiration and ambition. We hope that you continue to eat with us and thanks for your support so far. We have gone a long way and we think there is still a lot of room for improvement.

How to place an order at Organic Kitchen Shanghai?

To place an order you need to contact us via our wechat group, please find our QRcode in our contact section. Scan and join. We will send you our menu in our group chat and you will be able to place an order. Alternatively, you can place an order with our partners: Eleme, Meituan, JSS and Sherpas

where do you source your ingredients?

We source our ingredients from passionate organic farmers in Chongming island which is around one hour by car. Our farmers are organic certified and the island is isolated from polluted areas. This place is ideal for organic produce farming so we have selected very carefully farmers that best suits our needs. By sourcing our ingredients in proximity not only makes our food cheaper to buy but it also impacts our carbon footprint in a positive way. Therefore we benefit from our neutral carbon footprint and our food tastes amazing without chemicals that harm our body and our lands. We aim to contribute to a better world

How can I group order?

Please scan our Qrcode on our contact section, someone from our team will immediately add you and discuss the dishes and the quantities you wish to order. Large orders get 5% discount if your order is above 500 RMB.

Allergens

First of all, we have a fairly wide selection of vegetarian dishes in our central kitchen. Allergies and dietary requirements can also be shared with our staff in our Wechat group before you make an order. It is important, however, that in case of serious allergies, you look at the menu beforehand and of course indicate it to the waiters on this evening. We will do our best to serve our tapas in the most appropriate way.

How to eat at Organic Kitchen Shanghai?

Our culinary is baded on a mediterranean diet. We have a great range of foods that are vegan and vegeterian. We also have meat based dishes. Our food are cooked on the spot as fresh as possible. We offer appetizers, sandwiches, pasta, main dishes, soups, salads and home-made desserts. We have healthy smoothies, juices and coffees.
